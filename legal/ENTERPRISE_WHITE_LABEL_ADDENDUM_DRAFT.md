# Enterprise White-Label Addendum (Draft)

Last updated: 2026-04-25

This addendum draft supplements a base commercial agreement and is not legal advice.

## Parties and scope

This White-Label Addendum ("Addendum") is entered between Circaevum and Customer and applies only to Products listed in the applicable Order Form.

## 1) White-label grant

During the Subscription Term, and subject to full payment and compliance, Circaevum grants Customer a non-exclusive right to remove default "Powered by Circaevum" attribution within approved Products.

## 2) Brand usage limits

- Customer may not use Circaevum logos or other Circaevum Marks except as expressly permitted in writing.
- Customer may not imply endorsement, ownership, or partnership absent written approval.

## 3) Technical and compliance obligations

Customer must:

- Preserve underlying legal notices required by open-source licenses.
- Maintain a support contact for white-label deployment incidents.
- Comply with security and usage limits in the base agreement.

## 4) Support tier dependency

White-label rights are conditional on maintaining the agreed commercial tier and support package, if specified in the Order Form.

## 5) Revocation trigger events

Circaevum may suspend or revoke white-label rights for:

- Non-payment.
- Material breach of agreement.
- Trademark misuse or misleading branding.

## 6) Effect of termination

Upon expiration or termination of this Addendum, Customer must restore attribution and cease white-label presentation unless otherwise agreed in writing.

## 7) Conflict order

If this Addendum conflicts with the base agreement, this Addendum controls only for white-label subject matter.
